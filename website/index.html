About the NFT

🤓 ERC-1155

GMers is a custom, single-NFT ERC-1155 token contract with onchain royalty support and ERC-1155 enumeration. ERC-1155 contracts allow 'semi-fungible' tokens, and this project, on Base, takes non-fungible art to new levels of fungibility. +35k tokens were airdropped to the Mfers and BLONKS communities, and a very accessible (cheap) mint will last all summer.

☕ Artwork

With cup and brush, PoisonFemme sacrifices her own lifeblood (the coffee she's drinking) to paint GMers and other silly things. For each work, the artist notes very important details, like the coffee preparation method, the roast, if there were add-ins (creams/sweeteners), and if there happened to be any crumbs from her coffee-cookies on the 'canvas'.

✨ Contract

Consistent with Matto's "do more with less" and "why not both?" approaches to life and crypto, this contract manages a single NFT but it allows infinite art. Released under a MIT license, the contract can easily be modified and deployed for other, similar use cases. Royalties cannot be more than 5%, the contract includes an ERC-20-like totalSupply() function, and the contract supports EIP-2981.
